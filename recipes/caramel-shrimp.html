Introduction

Recipe Information

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 20 mins
  • Total: 30 mins
  • Servings: 4
Nutrition Facts (Per servings)
Energy 150kcal
Protein 24g
Carbohydrates 8g
Fat 2g

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 lb. (0.2 kg) fresh water prawn or shrimp, shell-on and head-on
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 bunch scallion

Directions

  1. In a small sauce pan on medium-low heat, add the sugar and water. Keep stirring until the sugar and water turn into a golden amber color caramel sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok at high heat. Add shallot and garlic and garlic, stir fry until aromatic but not browned.
  3. Add the shrimp and stir fry in a back-and-forth motions with a spatula.
  4. Add the Caramel Sauce, fish sauce and ground black pepper.
  5. Cook until the shrimp is cooked through and the sauce thickens, about 3 minutes. Add the scallions, stir, and turn off heat. Serve the Caramel Shrimp with rice immediately.
  6. Add the scallions, stir, and turn off heat. Serve the Caramel Shrimp with rice immediately.

Cook's Note

Don’t overcook your shrimp. Once they’re pink and starting to curl, take them off the heat. They will cook just a touch more once they’re tossed with the hot sauce.
