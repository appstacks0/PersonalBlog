Fried Tofu

Introduction

Recipe Information

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 20 mins
  • Total: 25 mins
  • Servings: 2
Nutrition Facts (2 servings)
Energy 213kcal
Protein 22g
Carbohydrates 8g
Fat 10g

Ingredients

  • 1 block tofu (medium-firm to firm)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Â¼ tablespoons thinly-sliced scallions, green parts (3-4 stalks)
  • Some cooking oil for frying
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Fry tofu in a pan until golden and set aside.
  2. Heat up a few tbs of light olive oil and then add and fry green onions until fragrant and cooked then remove heat.
  3. Add crushed black pepper into scallion oil.
  4. Drizzle black pepper/scallion oil over tofu.
  5. Add black pepper into 2 tbs fish sauce to make dipping sauce.

Cook's Note

To get the fried tofu crispy with the inside still soft, let's use a deep fry pan with a lot of hot oil.
